# Privacy Policy

**Incharj** is a local-first desktop application. Your privacy is straightforward:

## What we collect

Nothing. Incharj does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

## How your data is handled

- All file indexing happens **locally on your machine**
- Your search index is stored in a local SQLite database on your device
- Cloud integrations (Google Drive, Notion) connect directly from your machine to those services using your own OAuth credentials — no data passes through our servers

## Third-party services

When you connect Google Drive or Notion, you authorize Incharj to read your files directly from those services. Please refer to their respective privacy policies:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Notion Privacy Policy](https://www.notion.so/privacy)

## Contact

For questions, open an issue at [github.com/okumujustine/pgdoctor-cli](https://github.com/okumujustine/pgdoctor-cli/issues).
